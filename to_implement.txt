ULIDs are nice because:

unique
sortable by time
readable
distributed-safe

Install:

pip install ulid-py

Python:

import ulid

movie_id = str(ulid.new())

print(movie_id)

Output:

01JZ8K6R4J7A3N5M8K2XQ9YFZW

Pros:

sortable chronologically
good for databases/indexing
safer than sequential IDs
modern

A lot of newer systems prefer ULIDs over UUIDs.
